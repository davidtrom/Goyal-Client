The Process

A Tailor-Made Treatment

Your situation is unique. Dr. Goyal believes that your care should be too! Working in conjunction with each patient, Dr. Goyal is passionate about creating a comprehensive treatment plan for adult patients that is uniquely suited to their needs and situation. This may include medications as deemed necessary, along with various therapies including CBT, DBT and psychodynamic therapy. The approach is broken down into three phases:

1.
Initial Evaluation
In your first visit, Dr. Goyal will focus on identifying your needs. Together you will determine the most effective course of mdeication and therapy.
2.
Treatment Plan
In the next few sessions, Dr. Goyal will undertak a comprehensive assessment and together you will create a roadmap of your treatment and goals.
3.
Targeted Sessions
The remaining sessions will examine each issue or obstacle to get to the root of your problem. You will gain deeper understanding of the illness as an active participant in your recovery.
If you are a NEW patient making an appointment, please have the following information ready when you call to make your first appointment:
  • Complete Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Email Address
  • Photos of your ID
  • Photos of your insurance card (Front & Back)
Before your first visit, you will receive an email with a link and password to the Patient Portal, where you will be able to access all your patient documents.
