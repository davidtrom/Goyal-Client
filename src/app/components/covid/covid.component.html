Important Information Regarding Covid-19

At this time Dr. Goyal is only seeing patients in person on an as needed basis. If you are a patient being seen in person, Dr. Goyal is following all CDC guidelines in regards to social distancing. A link to to those guidelines can be found HERE. If you are not feeling well or have any symptoms of COVID-19, please reschedule your appointment and consult your primary healthcare provider for information on testing. Current and new patients that do not need in person appointments will conduct their appointments via telemedicine. Dr. Goyal is committed to helping her patients meet their needs even during the uncertainty of this crisis.
